WHAT WILL YOU DO EXACTLY?
A day in the life of a “WLZ nurse“ is utilizing the nursing process to assess, plan, implement and evaluate the care of our home health clients. You will support and implement the philosophy, goals and standards of the home health nursing department within Leven en Zorg. You must demonstrate knowledge of the principles of growth and development of the life span and possess the ability to assess data reflective of the patient's status. No day will be the same and you are for example skilled to act in an acute emergency situation in the home of your client. You shall display competence and render care/treatment for our clients. You will be responsible for case management of assigned patients in accordance with our policies and procedures and all other applicable procedure manuals, laws and relevant professional standards. You will always stay in direct contact with our Nursing coordinator.
